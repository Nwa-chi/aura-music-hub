# AURA Legal Music Readiness

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This is the rights checklist to complete before AURA carries real traffic.

## Required rights for every release

- Song/master recording owner approval.
- Publishing/composition rights where required.
- Sample, beat, loop, and instrumental permissions.
- Lyric display permission.
- Cover image, artist photo, and promotional image permission.
- Artist name, likeness, profile, and featured artist approval.
- Written uploader confirmation that they control the content or are authorized to upload it.

## Minimum release record

For every song, keep a private release record with:

- Song title.
- Artist and featured artists.
- Uploader email.
- Audio file source.
- Cover art source.
- Lyrics source.
- Rights owner or license contact.
- License type or written permission link.
- Date approved for AURA.
- Admin who approved it.

## High-risk content

Do not publish until reviewed:

- Remixes.
- Covers.
- Songs using famous beats or samples.
- AI-generated vocals that imitate a real artist.
- Artist photos pulled from search engines.
- Lyrics copied from public lyric sites.
- Music from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or streaming platforms without written permission.

## Recommended launch rule

Launch only with songs that are:

- Your own content.
- Directly licensed from the artist.
- Public domain recordings with verified recording rights.
- Creative Commons licensed for the exact commercial/noncommercial use AURA needs.

This is not legal advice. Have a music/media lawyer review AURA before scaling beyond private beta.
