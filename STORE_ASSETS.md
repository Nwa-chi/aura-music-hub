# AURA Store Assets Checklist

Last updated: July 1, 2026

## App store identity

- App name: AURA
- Website: https://www.auramusichub.com
- Support email: support@auramusichub.com
- Privacy policy: https://www.auramusichub.com/privacy
- Terms: https://www.auramusichub.com/terms
- Copyright/DMCA: https://www.auramusichub.com/dmca

## Suggested descriptions

Short description:

> AURA is a music streaming home for songs, lyrics, playlists, artist profiles, and personal mixes.

Long description:

> AURA helps listeners stream songs, follow artists, save favorites, read synchronized lyrics, browse playlists, and discover music through personal listening signals. Creators can upload songs, lyrics, cover images, and metadata for owner review before publishing. The owner dashboard includes release moderation, content reporting, deletion requests, audit logs, and version-aware publishing controls.

## Screenshots to capture

- Home screen with player visible.
- Search and collection tags.
- Song page/player with synchronized lyrics.
- Artist profile.
- Playlist or album collection.
- Account panel.
- Upload flow.
- Owner admin dashboard.

## Google Play feature graphic

- Size: 1024 x 500 px.
- Show the AURA brand and music experience.
- Avoid tiny text.
- Include real app screenshots or polished product visuals.

## Review/test account

Create a non-owner test account for store review:

- Email: review@auramusichub.com
- Access: listener account with saved favorites and no admin privileges.
- Notes: provide login instructions only inside the store submission form.

Create an owner/admin test account only if a reviewer specifically needs to inspect upload moderation.

## Age rating notes

Expected baseline: Teen or equivalent if user uploads, music content, and lyrics can include mature themes. Final rating depends on the songs you publish and whether explicit content is allowed.
